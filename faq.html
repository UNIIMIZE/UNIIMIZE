Digital Marketing General

What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing is promoting your business through online channels — search engines, social media, email, websites, and paid ads — to reach and convert your target audience.

Why does my business need digital marketing?

Your customers are online. Digital marketing helps you reach them where they are, build trust, generate leads, and grow revenue cost-effectively.

What is SEO and why does it matter?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) improves your website's visibility on Google. Higher rankings mean more organic traffic and free, long-term leads.

What is the difference between SEO and paid ads?

SEO is a long-term strategy that builds organic visibility over time. Paid ads deliver immediate visibility but stop the moment you stop spending.

What is social media marketing?

It's the use of platforms like Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and YouTube to build your brand, engage your audience, and drive traffic or sales.

What is a content marketing strategy?

It's a plan for consistently creating and distributing valuable content (blogs, videos, infographics) to attract, educate, and convert your target audience.

What is ROAS?

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) measures how much revenue you earn for every rupee/dollar spent on advertising. E.g., a ROAS of 4x means ₹4 earned per ₹1 spent.

What platforms do you run ads on?

Primarily Google Ads and Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram). We also work with YouTube, LinkedIn, and others depending on your audience.

How do I know which digital marketing service is right for my business?

That's what our free discovery call is for. We'll assess your goals, audience, and budget and recommend the best starting point.
