How to Protect a PDF

  • Upload the PDF you want to secure.
  • Enter a strong password in the "Set Password" field.
  • Confirm the password to avoid typos.
  • Click "Encrypt PDF" to apply 128-bit encryption.
  • Download your protected file immediately.

About This Tool

The PDFORY Protect tool allows you to encrypt your PDF documents with industry-standard encryption. This ensures that only people with the password can view the contents of your file.

Privacy Guarantee: The encryption process happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your file and your password are never sent to our servers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover the password if I forget it?

No. The encryption is standard PDF security. If you lose the password, the file cannot be opened. Please keep your password safe.

Is the encryption strong?

We use standard 128-bit AES encryption, which is sufficient for most business and personal needs.
