Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit on file size?

Since we process files in your browser, the limit depends on your device's memory. Most users can merge dozens of files easily.

Can I reorder pages inside the files?

This tool merges full files. To reorder pages, use the "Split PDF" or "Delete Pages" tools first.
