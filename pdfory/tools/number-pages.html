How to Number PDF Pages

  • Upload your PDF document above.
  • Choose where you want the page numbers to appear.
  • Verify the position on the preview.
  • Click "Add Numbers" to process all pages.
  • Download your new paginated PDF.

Features

Easily organize lengthy documents by adding orderly page numbers. Works on any PDF size or orientation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start numbering from a specific page?

Currently, we number all pages sequentially starting from Page 1.

Does this overwrite existing content?

The numbers are added as a new layer on top. Ensure you have margin space to avoid overlapping text.
