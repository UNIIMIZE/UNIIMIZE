How to Compress a PDF

  • Upload your PDF document to the optimizer.
  • Adjust the "JPEG Quality" slider to balance size and clarity.
  • Use the "Scale" option to resize the document dimensions if needed.
  • Check the preview to ensure it looks good.
  • Click "Compress & Download".

Why Compress PDFs?

Smaller files are easier to email, upload to websites, and store on your device. Our tool optimizes images and structure without ruining text readability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my text become blurry?

Text usually remains sharp as it is vector-based. Only images are compressed/pixelated based on your quality settings.

How much can I reduce the file size?

It depends on the content. Image-heavy PDFs can often be reduced by 50-80%.
