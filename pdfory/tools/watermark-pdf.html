How to Watermark a PDF

  • Upload your PDF document.
  • Type your watermark text (e.g., "DRAFT" or "CONFIDENTIAL").
  • Customize color and opacity to blend it with your document.
  • Click "Apply & Download" to stamp all pages.

Why Use Watermarks?

Watermarks protect your intellectual property and indicate the status of a document. They prevent unauthorized use and ensure receivers know the document's classification.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I remove the watermark later?

Watermarks are permanent layers added to the PDF. You would need the original file to have a clean version.

Does it cover text?

You can adjust the opacity (transparency) so the underlying text remains readable.
